# SanguisFalx

> *May the hatred of your enemies become your most powerful weapon.*

A NeoForge mod for Minecraft 1.21.1 that adds the **Sanguis Falx** — a powerful scythe forged from blood and dark energy, built for spellcasters and warriors alike.

---

## The Weapon

The **Sanguis Falx** is a unique scythe with custom stats and deep integration with the magic mod ecosystem:

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Attack Damage | 20 |
| Attack Speed | 2.0/sec |
| Durability | 8064 |
| Enchantability | 10 |

### Passive Attributes
- **+30% Critical Hit Chance**
- **+30% Spell Power**
- **+20% Spell Resistance**
- **+5 Entity Interaction Range**

---

## The IRA

The **Ira** is a unique curios item with custom stats and deep integration with the magic mod ecosystem:

| Stat              | Value   |
|-------------------|---------|
| Magic Proficiency | 20%     |
| Magic Resistance  | 20%  |

## Mod Compatibility

| Mod | Integration |
|---|---|
| [Malum](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/malum) | Recognized as a scythe and magic-capable weapon |
| [Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/irons-spells-n-spellbooks) | Can be imbued and upgraded |
| [Apothic Attributes](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/apothic-attributes) | Critical hit chance attribute support |
